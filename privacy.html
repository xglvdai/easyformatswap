Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

1. Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At easyformatswap, your privacy is our top priority. We built this platform with a simple philosophy: your files belong to you, and they should never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to share them.

2. How Our Tools Work

All file conversions — image conversion, PDF processing, audio/video transcoding, unit conversion, and file compression — are processed entirely within your web browser using client-side technologies such as JavaScript, WebAssembly, and browser-native APIs. Your files are never uploaded to any server.

This means:

  • No files are transmitted over the internet
  • No files are stored on any remote server
  • No third party can access your files during conversion
  • You can use our tools offline after the page loads

3. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information. Specifically:

3.1 Files You Convert

Files you drag, drop, or select for conversion are processed exclusively in your browser's memory (RAM) and are cleared when you close the tab or navigate away. We have no access to them.

3.2 Usage Data

We do not use analytics services, tracking scripts, or cookies that monitor your behavior. We do not collect IP addresses, browser fingerprints, session data, or any other form of personally identifiable information.

3.3 Cookies

easyformatswap does not set any cookies on your device. Our pages contain no third-party tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics cookies.

4. Third-Party Services

Our tools load open-source JavaScript and WebAssembly libraries from CDN providers (jsdelivr.net) to power conversion features such as libheif for HEIC decoding, pdf.js for PDF processing, pdf-lib for PDF generation, ffmpeg.wasm for media conversion, and browser-image-compression for image compression.

These libraries are loaded directly by your browser to perform conversions locally. The CDN provider may log the request for the library file itself as part of standard CDN operations, but your files are never sent to the CDN or any third party.

5. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. Since we collect no data at all, this is inherently compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

6. Data Security

Because all processing happens on your device and no data is ever transmitted to our servers, there is no risk of server-side data breaches affecting your files. The security of your data is managed by the security of your own device and browser.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including CCPA in California and GDPR in the EU/UK), you may have rights regarding your personal data. Since we collect and store no personal data, there is nothing to access, delete, or port. If you have any questions about your privacy rights in relation to our service, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please visit our Contact page to get in touch. We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.
